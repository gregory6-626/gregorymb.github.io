Manali & Spiti Expedition

REGION: TRANS-HIMALAYA · ELEVATION: ~3,800M · PROFILE: COLD ARID DESERT

Traveling through the high-altitude passes from Manali into the Spiti Valley reveals a stark transformation in landscape geometry. As the vegetation line falls away, the pure scale of the tectonic architecture becomes entirely visible. The thin air structures pristine conditions for capturing unscattered light across the valley plains.

At night, the lack of human infrastructure leaves the sky completely dark, providing views of the cosmos that match the calibration baselines of isolated scientific observatories.
