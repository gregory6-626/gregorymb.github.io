Nainital Interpretations

REGION: KUMAON LOWER HIMALAYAS · ASPECT: REFLECTIVE LAKE BASINS

Nainital provides an environment centered on geometric reflections and quiet landscape transitions. The mirror-like qualities of high-altitude lake basins create a unique medium for exploring symmetry and tracking changes during twilight hours.

Using long exposures allows the transient ripple features on the water's surface to blend into uniform gradients, mirroring the way digital sensors smoothly map incoming photons.
