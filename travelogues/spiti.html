Manali & Spiti Expedition

Region: Trans-Himalaya  ·  Elevation: ~3,800 m

Manali and Spiti valley are located in the Himachal Pradesh district of India. Notably Spiti is one of the highest villages in the world. Our journey started from Manali, seeing the greenery and then transitioning into high altitudes of Spiti. The Manali-Kaza route only opens up in June-September, and we went by HRTC bus. It was a one day journey and we could feel the altitude difference once we reached Spiti.
