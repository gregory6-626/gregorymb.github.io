Nainital & Devasthal

Region: Kumaon Lower Himalayas  ·  Elevation: ~2100 m

Nainital is a hill station located in the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand, India. It is known for its beautiful lakes and scenic landscapes. Devasthal is a nearby village known for its natural beauty and hosts the Devasthal Optical Telescope (DOT 3.6m, Asia's Largest Optical Telescope).
