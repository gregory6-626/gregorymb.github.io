The Abode of Clouds: Meghalaya

REGION: EAST KHASI HILLS · PROFILE: DENSE MIST & CANYONS · TYPE: HUMID ENVIRONMENT

Meghalaya offers an absolute atmospheric inversion to the northern dry plains. Here, thick cloud walls and heavy fog continuously roll through steep canyon breaks. The landscape functions under a constant flux of dynamic moisture tracking profiles.

Photographing this terrain requires tracking how soft, scattered light filters down through dense layers of vapor, highlighting waterfalls and historic root bridge networks below.
